Script for a training video on creating and tracking classes in a training management system

Training Course: Class Organizer Training

Outline

This training guide will help organizers manage training classes in XXX:

  • • Create a training class.
  • • Add participants to a class and invite them.
  • • Update class information and notify participants of changes.
  • • Complete a training class and mark participants as graduated.

What settings do I need to create a class?

  1. Open the Training Type form and select the type of training you want to use.
  2. In the Web Settings section, enable the options that should be available in the training management portal:
    • Enable participant invitations and notifications
    • Enable participant notes
    • Enable instructor features

How do I create a class?

  1. Open the Training Class form.
  2. Select New and choose the training type associated with the class.
  3. Complete all required fields.
  4. Enter the language, start time, and end time.
  5. Optionally enter a contact email address for participant communication.
  6. Enter the location where the class will be held.
  7. Add instructors or trainers who will have access to instructor features.
  8. Add any files or materials participants need for the class.
  9. Optionally generate the curriculum from the selected training type.

How do I add participants to a class?

  1. Open the Participant Assignment form.
  2. Search for eligible participants.
  3. Select the participants to add.
  4. Choose the newly created class.
  5. Select Add Participant.
  6. Select Assign to complete the enrollment.

Can I add an individual participant to a class?

Yes. To do so:

  1. Open the participant record or create a new one.
  2. Add the class in the participant's class enrollment section.

How do I invite participants to the class?

  1. Verify that all participants appear in the class roster.
  2. Select Send Email and choose the Participant Invitations option.
  3. An invitation email containing class details is generated and sent.

How do participants confirm or decline attendance?

Using the training portal, participants can:

  1. Accept or decline the class invitation.
  2. Optionally provide comments or notes for the organizer before the class starts.

How does the organizer review participant comments?

  1. Search for participant notes using the available search criteria.
  2. Open the participant record and mark notes as acknowledged after review.

What happens when the class is updated with a minor change?

Minor changes include updates such as contact information or location details. Participants are not required to take further action.

The organizer should send an update notification so participants are informed of the change.

What happens when the class is updated with a major change?

Major changes include updates to the class date or time. Participants who previously confirmed attendance may be required to review and confirm their attendance again.

The organizer should send an update notification informing participants of the revised schedule.

What do I do if the class is canceled?

Send a cancellation notification to all participants. A notification is also generated within the training portal.

No further participant action is required after the cancellation is communicated.

How do I complete a class and how does this affect participants?

Once the class is finished, the instructor or trainer should:

  1. Update attendance records for all participants.
  2. Enter any final completion notes.
  3. Select Complete Class to finalize the class.

Completing the class marks it as completed and records successful participation in each participant's training history.

How do I get more information?

Refer to the available help documentation and user guides for additional information.
